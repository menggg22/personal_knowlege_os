# Learning Quarterly

3-month strategic review. Zoom out.

## Usage

- `/learning-quarterly` — run at end of quarter

## Execution

1. **Read all monthly reviews** from the quarter: `JOURNAL/reviews/*.md`
2. **Scan knowledge growth**: count new entries per topic in `KNOWLEDGE/`
3. **Ask user**:
   - Where do you want to be in 6 months?
   - What trends are you seeing in your field?
   - Are you heading in the right direction?
4. **Write quarterly review**:
   - 3-month trajectory (what topics deepened, what's new)
   - Patterns across months
   - Market alignment (is what you're learning in demand?)
   - Course correction recommendations
